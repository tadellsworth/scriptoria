About Confiteor

This small companion gathers the hours for the Sacrament of Reconciliation at Catholic parishes within roughly a thirty-minute drive of downtown Canton, Georgia. It is intended as a quick reference — always confirm with the parish bulletin or website, especially during Advent, Lent, and Holy Week.

How to Use

The Next Opportunity banner shows the soonest confession available today or in coming days. The church list can be filtered by day of week or sorted by distance. Tap any card to see the full weekly Mass, Confession, and Adoration schedules, directions, call button, and website link.

Times highlighted in gold mark the next upcoming confession for that parish. A green Now badge means confessions are happening right now. A sage Adoration now indicator under the parish city means the Blessed Sacrament is currently exposed.

A Note on Accuracy

Mass, Confession, and Adoration schedules change — especially during liturgical seasons, Holy Week, and parish holidays. This app uses pre-populated data that should be verified periodically. When in doubt, call ahead. The phone number and website are always one tap away.

An Act of Contrition

O my God, I am heartily sorry for having offended Thee, and I detest all my sins, because I dread the loss of heaven, and the pains of hell; but most of all because they offend Thee, my God, Who art all good and deserving of all my love. I firmly resolve, with the help of Thy grace, to confess my sins, to do penance, and to amend my life. Amen.
