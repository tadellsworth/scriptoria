☧  THE SCRIPTORIA SUITE

One Super App, five companions

The Little Missal, Lingua Latina, Credo, Confiteor, and Laudate — dissolved into a single illuminated companion under one consistent Scriptoria look.

The merge · information architecture

The five apps overlap (readings, confession, and catechism each appear in more than one). I've resolved them into five tabs, each absorbing the apps that belong to it:

TodayDaily readings + the Little Missal devotion
PrayPrayer library, Rosary, Liturgy of the Hours, Examen
LearnCredo catechism path + Lingua Latina flashcards
FindConfiteor — Mass, Confession & Adoration near you
MeQuiet streak, journal, confession tracker, progress
The system · what stays constant
  • Parchment ground, lapis & gilt. One palette everywhere — Credo's gamified chrome and Confiteor's dark theme are retired into the unified light look.
  • Three voices. Cinzel titles, Cormorant prose, Outfit chrome.
  • Gentle, not gamified. Hearts and XP are gone; a single quiet streak (✦) is all that remains.
  • Masthead & gilt rule open every screen; the Chi-Rho is the constant mark.

Below: three directions for the home screen, then every tab as a finished screen.
