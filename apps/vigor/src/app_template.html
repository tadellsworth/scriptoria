How the hour works

Every weekday follows the same six-block frame: warm-up mobility, lymph activation, the day's strength work, a kegel set, a core finisher, and a cooldown stretch. The strength focus rotates so the whole body gets worked across the week.

Before you begin. This is a general fitness routine, not medical advice. Check with your doctor before starting if you have heart, joint, blood-pressure, or other health concerns.
